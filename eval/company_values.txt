Company Values — Meridian AI

We are a small, high-ownership team building production ML systems for enterprise clients. We value the following:

1. Ownership over process
   We expect engineers to take end-to-end ownership of problems — from research to deployment to monitoring. We do not hand off. We do not wait for permission. If you see a problem, you fix it.

2. Clarity in communication
   We write clearly. We document decisions. We give precise, evidence-based feedback. Vague answers ("it depends", "kinda works") are not acceptable in code reviews, design docs, or client calls.

3. Pragmatic engineering
   We ship. We do not over-engineer. A working, tested solution beats an elegant unfinished one. We prototype fast, measure, and iterate. We are not afraid to throw things away.

4. Deep technical craft
   We care about the quality of our work. We read papers. We understand the systems we use. We do not cargo-cult solutions. We are expected to know why, not just how.

5. Low ego, high impact
   We do not value seniority signals. We value impact. The best idea wins regardless of who proposed it. We are direct, respectful, and assume good intent.

6. Customer obsession
   We build for real users solving real problems. We talk to customers regularly. We treat production incidents with urgency. We do not ship things we would not use ourselves.
